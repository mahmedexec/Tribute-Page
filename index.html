Sir. Winston Churchill

Here are a few important moments from Sir. Churchill's lifetime:

  • 1874 - Winston Churchill is born in Oxfordshire.
  • 1894 - Churchill graduates from the Royal Military College in Sandhurst.
  • 1899 - Churchill campaigns for Parliament.
  • 1900 - Churchill wins a seat in the British House of Commons.
  • 1908 - Churchill marries Clementine Hozier.
  • 1940 - Churchill begins first term as prime minister.
  • 1945 - Churchill attends the Yalta Conference.
  • 1945 - Churchill loses the United Kingdom general election.
  • 1946 - Churchill gives his famous "iron curtain" speech.
  • 1951 - Churchill is elected prime minister again.
  • 1953 - Churchill wins the Nobel Prize for literature.
  • 1955 - Churchill retires as prime minister.
  • 1965 - Winston Churhill dies.
